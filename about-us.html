About Us

Reispar Analytics Academy is a community driven eco-system in Data Analytics and Artificial Intelligence. The Academy is an emerging leader in driving emerging technologies via products, solutions, projects, programmes among others.

As part of our open-source community project, we grouped a team professionals who volunteered their time and expertise in providing available insights to the world on Covid19 (Corona Virus).

This real time Analytics web page on the Corona virus is made available from Reispar Academy to world organisations and governments. It's our hope that it will serve for good governance.

Reispar Analytics Academy organises technology training in Data Science, Analytics, Machine Learning, among others for businesses, industries and individuals.

This webpage shows live insights on Covid19 across the world. Learn more about our activities here medium.com/@reisparanalyticsacademy
