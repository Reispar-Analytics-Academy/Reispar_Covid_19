Confirmed cases of Covid19 reached about 3 million globally in April 2020, staying safe cannot be over emphasized upon. Businesses across the globe have had to cope with the loss of revenue as well as disrupted market and supply chain services.


Let's Join Hands Together to SLOW THE SPREAD OF THE CORONAVIRUS!

Kindly follow these guidelines as backed by the World Health Organization:

Stay at home if you can and avoid any non-essential travel. Avoid social gatherings of more than 10 people pending when your government or local health authority state otherwise.

Yes, Practice social distancing by keeping at least 2 meters; about two arm lengths — away from others if you must go out in public.

Avoid close contact with people who are sick. Virtual communication is massively encouraged.

Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, most especially after being in a public place, or after blowing your nose, coughing or sneezing. If soap and water are not readily available, use a hand sanitizer with at least 60% alcohol.

Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth with unwashed hands.

Clean and disinfect household surfaces daily, regularly used devices and surfaces frequently throughout the day.

Cover your coughs and sneezes. Use a tissue to cover your nose and mouth and throw used tissues in a lined waste can. If a tissue isn't available, cough or sneeze into your elbow — not your hands. Wash your hands immediately.
